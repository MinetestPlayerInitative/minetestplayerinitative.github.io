About

MPI is a group about getting people to play FOSS games. Minetest is a great subject for this. We are showcasing mods, showing people why they should play FOSS games, and encouraging a more open-source future.
Join us at minetest fourm post if you want to join.
You can play our server @ Doopy The Server. All of our showcased mods are installed there.
