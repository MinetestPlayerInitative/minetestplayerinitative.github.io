Why Minetest?

Minetest has several great features! Here are some features that some voxel building games don't have, that we do:
  • Built in mod support
  • Sub game support
  • Download mods on join
  • Serverlist
  • Sub accounts on servers
  • Multiple terrain generators
  • Cross-Platform multiplayer (Android version works with PC servers + mods
  • ... and more
